On-line MEM Internship Resume Book

Students who are seeking an ENGG 390 internship/project for summer 2009, and who will graduate from the MEM program by June 2010, may be included in this resume book. The book is password protected, and access is given only to members of the MEM Corporate Collaboration Council and employers who call specifically interested in MEM students for internships this summer.

If you are not yet included, and wish to be, email to Robert Graves (Robert.J.Graves@Dartmouth.edu) and Engineering Career Services (Engineering.Career.Services@Dartmouth.EDU) the following:

  1. Please include me as seeking the following type of internship (choose only 1):
    • business
    • combination business & engineering
    • engineering
  2. Specific areas of interest
  3. Preferred location
  4. Term seeking internship
  5. Expected graduation date
  6. A short statement describing what you are looking for in an internship (this should be just a few sentences and will be posted exactly how you write it)
  7. Your resume
