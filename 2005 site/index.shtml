Engineering 390 - Master of Engineering Management Project

ENGG 390 is a required course in the M.E.M. program, during which students complete an individual project for a corporate sponsor. There are no class meetings, and the intent is to give the students authentic experience working on real-world projects. Sponsors benefit from the hard work, expertise, and outside perspective of the students.
